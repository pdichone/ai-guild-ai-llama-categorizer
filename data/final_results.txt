Recipe Name: Summer Squash Salad

Ingredients:
 - 1 medium zucchini
 - 1 medium yellow squash
 - 1/4 cup olive oil
 - 2 tablespoons lemon juice
 - Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
Step 1: Preheat oven to 400Â°F. Step 2: Slice the zucchini and yellow squash into thin rounds. Step 3: Toss with olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper on a baking sheet. Step 4: Roast for 20-25 minutes or until tender. Step 5: Let cool completely before assembling the salad.
